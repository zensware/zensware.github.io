Community rules and prohibited content for the zensware Discord server

By participating in our Discord server, you agree to follow these rules. Violations may result in warnings, mutes, kicks, or permanent bans at staff discretion.

1

No Harassment or Hate Speech

Do not harass, bully, or discriminate against anyone based on race, ethnicity, gender, sexual orientation, religion, disability, or any other protected characteristic.

2

No Malicious Software

Do not share, promote, or discuss malware, viruses, ransomware, or any software designed to harm computers or networks.

3

No Doxxing or Personal Information

Never share someone's personal information without their explicit consent. This includes real names, addresses, phone numbers, emails, or any private details.

4

No Spam or Flooding

Do not spam messages, emojis, images, or @mentions. Avoid excessive caps, copy-pasting, or disruptive behavior in text or voice channels.

5

No Cheating Discussion in Public Channels

Do not publicly discuss specific cheating methods, bypass techniques, or ways to circumvent security measures. Keep such discussions to appropriate private channels if allowed.

6

No NSFW or Inappropriate Content

Do not post sexually explicit, graphically violent, or otherwise inappropriate content. This includes profile pictures, usernames, and statuses.

7

No Advertising or Self-Promotion

Do not advertise other servers, products, services, or social media without explicit permission from staff. This includes DM advertising to members.

8

No Impersonation

Do not impersonate staff members, developers, or other community members. This includes using similar usernames, nicknames, or profile pictures to deceive others.

9

No Discussion of Illegal Activities

Do not discuss, plan, or promote illegal activities including but not limited to hacking, fraud, theft, or distribution of illegal content.

10

No Evading Punishments

Do not use alternate accounts to evade bans, mutes, or other punishments. This will result in all associated accounts being permanently banned.

These rules are subject to change. Staff members have final say in rule interpretation and enforcement. When in doubt, ask a moderator.
