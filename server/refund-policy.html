Refund Policy

Our guidelines for refunds, customer support, and issue resolution

Important: Most of our software is available for free. This policy primarily applies to any paid services, donations, or premium features we may offer.

General Refund Policy

We stand behind the quality of our software. However, due to the digital nature of our products and services, we have specific refund policies in place:

Digital Products

Digital software purchases are typically non-refundable once the download link has been accessed or the product has been delivered.

Service Issues

If our software is fundamentally non-functional or fails to operate as advertised, we will work with you to resolve the issue or provide a refund.

Donations

All donations are non-refundable as they are considered voluntary contributions to support development.

Eligibility for Refunds

Refunds may be considered under the following circumstances:

  • Technical Issues: The software fails to work on a system meeting minimum requirements
  • False Advertising: The product does not perform as explicitly described on our website
  • Duplicate Charges: You were accidentally charged multiple times for the same product
  • Unauthorized Purchase: The purchase was made without your consent or by a minor

Non-Refundable Situations

We cannot provide refunds in these cases:

  • Change of mind after purchase
  • Failure to read system requirements before purchase
  • Incompatibility with third-party software or hardware
  • Violation of our Terms of Service leading to access revocation
  • Purchases made more than 14 days ago
  • Digital products that have been downloaded, accessed, or used
  • Issues arising from user error or improper configuration

Warning: Attempting to initiate chargebacks or fraudulent refund requests will result in permanent ban from all our services and may lead to legal action.

How to Request a Refund

If you believe you're eligible for a refund, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact Support First: Reach out to our support team with detailed information about your issue
  2. Provide Evidence: Include screenshots, error messages, and system information
  3. Allow Time for Resolution: We typically respond within 24-48 hours
  4. Follow Instructions: Our support team may provide troubleshooting steps before approving a refund

Tip: Most technical issues can be resolved through our support channels. Contact us before requesting a refund.

Refund Processing

If your refund is approved:

  • Refunds are processed within 5-10 business days
  • The refund will be issued to your original payment method
  • You will receive email confirmation when the refund is processed
  • Access to the software/service will be revoked immediately upon refund approval
